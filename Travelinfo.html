Hotel and Travel Info

Hotel:


ICOPS 2018 will be held at the Sheraton Denver Downtown at 1550 Court Pl., Denver,CO, 80202.
The conference will run from Sunday, June 24 (registration and welcome reception) through Thursday, June 28 (end of the technical program). Additionally, there will be a 1.5-day mini-course held Thursday afternoon and all-day Friday, June 28-29.


Air Travel to Denver:


Denver is served by Denver International Airport (Airport code DEN)


Getting from the Airport to the Hotel:


The “University of Colorado A-Line” is a new light rail service that connects Denver International Airport to Union Station in downtown Denver. A one-way ticket costs $9, and trains run every 15 minutes.

Once at Union Station, use the free MallRide to get to the hotel (ride from Union Station to Court Place). The MallRide is a free bus service that runs up and down the 16 th Street walking mall (all day, 7 days a week).The MallRide has 36 buses servicing the roughly 1-mile of the 16 th Street mall, and they come by every minute or two.
